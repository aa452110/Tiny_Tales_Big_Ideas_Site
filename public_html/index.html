Tiny Tales, Big Ideas

Transforming complex ideas into accessible adventures for everyone.

At Tiny Tales, Big Ideas, we are dedicated to making complex topics engaging, accessible, and fun. Our books, written by licensed educators, combine imaginative storytelling, AI-generated illustrations, and practical teaching suggestions to create a lasting reference for educational standards. Whether used at home, in the classroom, or simply for enjoyment, our stories provide teachers with a strong starting point for tackling challenging concepts and show learners of all ages that simplifying difficult ideas is a powerful way to expand knowledge.

About the Author

Brian Duryea is a public educator who has introduced thousands of students to the wonders of computer science. Inspired by a need to make complex ideas accessible, he developed the Tiny Tales, Big Ideas Computer Science series. By blending entertaining narratives with practical examples, Brian helps young readers grasp fundamental concepts so they can navigate the digital world with confidence.
